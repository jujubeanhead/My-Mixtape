Fearofdark - Rolling Down The Steet, In My Katamari

I believe this is a good introductory song for an average andy getting into chiptunes. The name is quite a mouthful but it is still a banger regardless.

Blue Navi - Groovy Raygun

This song blew my mind the first time I heard it, despite already having my dopamine receptors be fried at the time of discovery.

Zackery Wilson - FM2K

Chiptunes can be made with either trackers or something more mainstream like FL Studio. As long as it's got that chiptune vibe!

Agargara - Big Steely Escape

The big bumpin' drums on this song go so hard.

Cryptolovans - Fog Machine

I wouldn't blame anyone if they didn't like this track because it's the chiptune equivalent of an experimental jazz song. I think it's wonderful though.

Enacostione - Bubblegun

This one's very obscure, I wish this dude got more recognition. They make some beautiful stuff.

Lukas Eriksson - Forbidden Expressions

This one I discovered during high school. Made me quite happy during those dark times.

Potato-tan - welc.moe

I personally think this song is the best example of telling a story without saying a single word. Beautiful song.

Sabrepulse - A Girl I Know

Sabrepulse was the first chiptune artist I ever listened to. When I saw that video on Youtube of a butterfly on someone's hand with Sabrepulse music playing in the back, I was never the same ever again.

Anamanaguchi - Stay Home

A more recent tune from an iconic chiptune band. These are THE chiptune guys.
